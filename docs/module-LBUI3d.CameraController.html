Class: CameraController

LBUI3d.CameraController(worldLimitsopt, localLimitsopt) → {module:LBUI3d.CameraController}

new CameraController(worldLimitsopt, localLimitsopt) → {module:LBUI3d.CameraController}

Base class for an object that controls a camera. Typical camera controllers are associated with an module:LBGeometry.Object3D, which we call the target.

Camera controllers normally work within the context of an module:LBUI3d.View3D.

Depending upon the controller the camera may be panned or rotated.

The camera controllers are loosely based upon the camera controllers found in ThreeJS's examples/js/controls folder, such as OrbitControls.js and FirstPersonControls.js.
