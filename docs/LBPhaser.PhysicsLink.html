Class: PhysicsLink

LBPhaser.PhysicsLink

new PhysicsLink(phaserEnv) → {LBPhaser.PhysicsLink}

Base class that manages linking a LBPhysics.RigidBody to a physics engine for use with Phaser.

This also supports the display of force arrows (via LBPhaser.Arrow objects) and updating sprites from their associated rigid bodies.
